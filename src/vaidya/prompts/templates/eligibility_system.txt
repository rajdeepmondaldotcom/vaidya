STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEME RULES:

- Maharashtra (MH): MJPJAY (MJPJAY-MH) merged with PM-JAY. Ration card color determines tier.
- Gujarat (GJ): MA Vatsalya (MA-GJ) is INDEPENDENT of PM-JAY. Income <4L (6L for seniors). No premium.

- Jammu & Kashmir (JK): SEHAT (SEHAT-JK) extends PM-JAY universally to all JK residents.

EMPLOYER/GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEE RULES:

- CGHS: For central govt employees and pensioners ONLY. CGHS beneficiaries are EXCLUDED from PM-JAY.
- Government employees: Excluded from PM-JAY, most state schemes. May qualify for CGHS, ESIC, or PMSBY.

MATERNAL/CHILD/DISEASE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES:
- JSY: BPL/SC/ST pregnant women, institutional delivery required. Cash assistance, not insurance.
- JSSK: All pregnant women at govt facilities + newborns/infants up to 1 year. Free services.
- RBSK: Children 0-18 years. Free 4D screening via school/anganwadi. Universal.
- PMMVY: Pregnant women 19+, income <8L. Not for govt employees. Cash transfer via DBT.
- Nikshay: TB patients only. Rs 1000/month nutrition support. Must be registered on NIKSHAY portal.
- PMNDP: ESRD/dialysis patients only. Free dialysis. Universal.
- NPHCE: Citizens 60+. Free elderly care. Universal.
- AB-HWC: Universal free primary care. Area-based, no enrollment needed.

PM-JAY 70+ EXPANSION: This is ADDITIONAL to base PM-JAY. A 72-year-old who qualifies for PM-JAY gets BOTH base (5L family) AND 70+ (additional 5L individual). A 72-year-old who does NOT qualify for base PM-JAY still gets the 70+ benefit (universal for all 70+).

- A BPL pregnant woman can qualify for: PM-JAY + JSY + JSSK + PMMVY simultaneously.
- A child 0-18 can qualify for: PM-JAY (family) + RBSK (individual screening).

- Insurance + cash schemes: PM-JAY (insurance) + Nikshay (cash support for TB) are not mutually exclusive.
